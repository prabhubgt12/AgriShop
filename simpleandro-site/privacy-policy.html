Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04 Sep 2025

Overview

Ledger Book (“the App”) stores your ledger data locally on your device. Optional cloud backup/restore uses your Google Drive account with your consent.

Data We Collect

  • Ledger data: Stored locally on your device. Not sent to our servers.
  • Google Drive (optional): If you sign in and back up, the App uploads files to a private folder in your Google Drive (scope: drive.file).
  • Advertising: AdMob may collect device and ad interaction data per Google policies.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core ledger features locally.
  • Perform backup/restore to your Google Drive when you request it.
  • Show ads (if enabled) via AdMob.

Third‑Party Services

  • Google Drive API (for backup/restore)
  • Google AdMob (ads)

Security

Your data remains on your device or your Google Drive. We do not operate backend servers for your content.

Your Choices

  • Use the App without Google sign‑in.
  • Disable ads by purchasing “Remove Ads” if available.
  • Delete backups from your Google Drive at any time.
